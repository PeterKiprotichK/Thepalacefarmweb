About The Palace Farm

At The Palace Farm, Kenya's leading agricultural innovation center, we revolutionize farming through smart technology, sustainable practices, and comprehensive agricultural training. Our expertise spans hydroponic farming, precision agriculture, poultry management, and modern cultivation techniques that maximize yields while preserving environmental integrity.

We specialize in transforming traditional farming methods with cutting-edge agricultural solutions including vertical gardens, greenhouse technology, organic farming systems, and integrated pest management. Our comprehensive approach to sustainable agriculture ensures food security and profitable farming ventures.

From premium Kienyeji poultry and Black Soldier Fly farming to advanced hydroponic systems and agricultural consultancy, we provide complete farming solutions that drive agricultural success across Kenya and East Africa.
