Farm Overview

Discover the heart of sustainable agriculture at The Palace Farm

Founded in 2012

In the heart of Kericho County, Kenya

The Palace Farm was founded in 2012 in the heart of Kericho County. Our mission is to blend traditional farming values with modern, smart agriculture to create a thriving, sustainable environment for healthy living and community empowerment.

We strive to deliver the freshest produce and quality livestock, while promoting knowledge-sharing and innovation among our neighbors, schools, and the next generation of farmers.

Innovation

Embracing smart farming technologies like vertical gardens and precision irrigation to boost yields and reduce waste.

Sustainability

Committed to eco-friendly practices, organic farming, and resource conservation for a greener future.

Technology

Using data, automation, and modern equipment to make farming smarter, safer, and more efficient.
