Our Story

Who We Are Passionate About Sustainable Farming

The Palace Farm is a dynamic agribusiness located in Kipsolu, Kericho, Kenya. We specialize in smart farming and provide a wide range of products including ornamental birds, poultry, dairy goats, local vegetables, seeds and seedlings, and landscaping services.

With over 12 years of experience in sustainable agriculture, our team is passionate about producing clean, organic vegetables and making them accessible to both urban and rural communities.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional farming and modern agricultural innovation by embracing sustainable practices and technology. From our carefully curated livestock to our lush vertical garden farm, we ensure that every product and service is delivered with care and excellence.

Our Journey & Future Plans

Over the past 12 years, The Palace Farm has grown from a local produce supplier into a fully-fledged smart farming operation. We've hosted agricultural learning tours for students, including those from Kesagetiet Junior Secondary School, to promote hands-on learning. Looking ahead, we are expanding into beekeeping, rabbit farming, and pig rearing with an aim to export Kenya's finest agricultural products to global markets.

2012: Humble Beginnings

Started as a small local vegetable supplier, focused on providing fresh produce to local families and markets with dedication to quality.

2016: Growth & Sustainability

Expanded our product range to include dairy goats, ornamental birds, and sustainable landscaping services, embracing eco-friendly practices.

2024 & Beyond

Focused on smart farming innovations, and global market expansion to showcase Kenya's agricultural excellence.

Looking Ahead

We are dedicated to leveraging technology and collaboration to elevate Kenyan agriculture. Our future plans include climate-smart farming practices, digital farm management systems, and expanding our educational initiatives to empower the next generation of farmers and agri-innovators.
